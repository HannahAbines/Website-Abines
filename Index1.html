ABOUT OUR RESTAURANT

Abines Restaurant was built by an incredibly ambitious woman starting this company. This company started in a tiny rented storage space somewhere in Taguig City, Metro Manila in the year 2022. At Abines Restaurant, we’re serving up more than a business restaurant. In fact, Abines Rrestaurant famous recipe is one of our unexpected specialties. Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than four decades, is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it’s topped with Abines signature herb-garlic butter, then carved tableside. The authentic taste comes from family recipes, and from fresh, healthy, simple and tasteful ingredients as most of it plant-based-food straight from home!

LOCATION OF OUR RESTAURANT

One of Filipinos's most beloved restaurants, Abines Restaurant has welcomed guests to enjoy its contemporary Filipino cuisine, warm hospitality, and unparalleled service in Taguig City for a year. Chef Lito Abines ever-evolving seasonal menu showcases the restaurant's relationships with local farms and purveyors. This restaurant was opened in 2022 by restaurateur Hannah Abines in a famous landmark building just near Mercado De Lago, the restaurant has earned nine James Beard Awards, including "Outstanding Restaurant" and "Outstanding Chef in Fiipino.” The exact location of this beloved is at Purok 6-C Lower Bicutan, Taguig City.
